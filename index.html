ENERGY OUT OF THE BOX
ABOUT US
We are enabling power companies to do grid balancing at optimal cost and with zero environmental harm
PROBLEM
Balancing the grid during peak consumption leads to excessive use of fossil fuels, which negatively affects the environment and imposes excessive costs for energy companies
SOLUTION
Algorithm stores cheaply produced energy in the battery and gives it back to the grid in the moments of peak demand which effectively balances grid and also prevents the usage of fossil fuels
